Smart Demand Site Management And Home Automation

This system works on the consumer sector that makes the controlling of our household appliances smarter and also manages the load on the basis of priority during peak hour and hence saves some electrical energy .

Demo

Abstract

This project presents the overall design of Home Automation System with low cost and wireless system. This system is designed to assist and provide support in order to fulfil the needs and requirement of individual who are busy and donâ€™t have much time for every work and also the people who are physically disabled can control appliances of their home easily . Also, the smart home concept in the system improves the standard living at home. The switch mode is used to control the home appliances. The state of home appliances is received as feedback in the android application. Also by using smart metering concept, we know the billing and power consumption of the house in our smart phone

Similarly, this project is designed to reduce the peak load. During the peak load time less prioritized devices are cut-off and only higher prioritized devices will run. This helps to save and manage energy during peak hour. There-by contributing in the power cut-off problem in Nepal. The main control system implements wireless technology to provide remote access from smart phone. The design remains the existing electrical switches and provides more safety control on the switches with low voltage activating method. The switches status is synchronized in all the control system whereby every user interface indicates the real time existing switches status. The system intended to control electrical appliances and devices in house with relatively low-cost design, user-friendly interface and ease of installation. Long term benefits could include energy savings by automatically ensuring lights and electronics are turned off. And equivalent power verses time graph is shown in the display unit.

 It is hoped this study will inform practitioners about the IOT controlled home automation along peak load management system with the smart metering concept.
